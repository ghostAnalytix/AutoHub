About AutoHub

Learn more about our journey and mission.

Our History

AutoHub was founded in 1990 with a vision to provide top-notch automotive parts and accessories to car enthusiasts and professionals alike. Over the years, we have grown from a small local shop to a leading national supplier, known for our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

From the very beginning, our goal has been to make auto parts accessible and affordable to everyone. We started with a small team of passionate car lovers and have since expanded to include experts from all areas of the automotive industry. Our journey has been fueled by a love for cars and a dedication to excellence.

Our Mission

At AutoHub, our mission is to provide high-quality auto parts and accessories at competitive prices, backed by exceptional customer service. We strive to be a trusted partner for car owners, mechanics, and automotive businesses, helping them to keep their vehicles running smoothly and efficiently.

We believe in continuous improvement and innovation, always looking for ways to better serve our customers and meet their evolving needs. Our extensive product range, knowledgeable staff, and user-friendly online store are all part of our commitment to making AutoHub the best place to shop for auto parts.
